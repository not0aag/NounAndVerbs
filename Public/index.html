How to Play

Learn Hindi words by identifying whether they are nouns or verbs!

  • Click 'Noun' or press 'N' for nouns
  • Click 'Verb' or press 'V' for verbs
  • Need help? Click 'Hint' or press 'H'
  • You have 3 attempts per game
  • Score points to reach higher levels
